**Wang, H.\*, Lu, Y.\*, & Yang, C. Y.\*** (2025). Predicting the Next Pitch Type and Analyzing Batter-Pitcher Strategic Interactions. *Connecticut Sports Analytics Symposium (CSAS 2025)*, Yale University, New Haven, CT.

Built predictive models (Random Forest, BART) using MLB data to classify pitch types and evaluate strategic interaction between batters and pitchers. Identified quasi-causal effects of extreme bat-speed observations on pitcher fastball decisions, revealing instability in strategy selection under high-variance conditions.
