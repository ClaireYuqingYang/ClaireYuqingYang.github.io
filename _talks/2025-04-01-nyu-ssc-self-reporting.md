**Yang, C. Y.\*, & Lu, Y.\*** (2025). When Professionals Stretch the Truth: Exploring Inflated Self-Reporting in Social Surveys. *2025 NYU Society for Statistics Conference*, New York, NY. Chapter of the American Statistical Association.

Investigated self-reporting bias using General Social Survey (N=13,932). Used inverse probability weighting with boosted regression to correct for group-level bias. Found professional overstatement of educational achievements (~24%, p<.01) in self-reporting.
